Lemon Cheesecake

Home

Description

This lemon cheesecake recipe is easy to throw together for a smooth and creamy summer dessert. You can't even tell it is made with low-fat ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Reduced Fat Graham Cracker Pie Crust: 1 (9 inches)
  • Cream cheese: 2 packages (8 ounces)
  • White sugar: 1/2 cup
  • All-purpose Flour: 1 1/2 tbsp
  • Egg Substitute: 1/2 cup
  • Lemon Juice: 2 1/2 tbsp
  • Nonfat Lemon Yogurt: 1 container (8 ounces)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Place pie crust on a baking sheet.
  2. Beat cream cheese and sugar in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth; beat in flour, then egg substitute and lemon juice. Stir in lemon yogurt until combined; pour mixture into crust.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until filling is set, about 50 to 60 minutes. Allow pie to cool, then refrigerate at least 4 hours before serving.
